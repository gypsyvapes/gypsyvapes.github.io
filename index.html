Vision starter kit

It first was released in 2015. Starter pack contains the following things 


Protank-II (Version 2) Clearomizer, 1*1600mAh VV Battery for that Vision spinner 3, USB Cable for charging, Power adapter for United States standards, User guide on English. 


Whole package is very very plain. Overall weight for whole product is like 123 gram which is close to 0 so you can be happy with it. CONNECTION THREADING is 510 which is also like standard number for this. Wattage is not variable but voltage is and is from 3.4 to 4.8. Coil replaceable but not rebuildable here. Whole TANK made of glass material.


Color of that vape pen is black by itself. Main material done from stainless steel with minor parts are not. Secondary colors are like golden plus green plates. Other materials are also brass, glass and carbon fiber one. 


Battery, let's talk about it. You need to press button like five times to turn on or turn off. LED color is white in case you are interested. Also since it's contain that battery shipping options are limited because of it so you should be aware of that. 


Full product name - Protank II (V2) / Vision Spinner III Styled 1600mAh VV Rechargeable Starter Kit. If you buy many you will be eligible for discounts. It's also compatible with eGo batteries and eGo threading mods. You shouldn't overcharge the battery or leave it without attention for a very long time in other words.
